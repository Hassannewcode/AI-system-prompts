# AI System Prompts and Architectures
A curated repository documenting AI system prompts and structural architectures retrieved directly from various AI models through strategic prompting, iterative loops, and contextual exploration.
> **Notice:** All documents in this repository consist of active system instructions extracted directly from the models. These files were not obtained via web scraping or backend API breaches. Instead, they were recovered by interacting with the AI models using advanced, layered prompting techniques.
---
## Important Notice & Context
The prompts documented in this repository, despite their structural appearance, are not officially published or verified by their respective authoritative developers. They were generated and disclosed by artificial intelligence systems interacting under specific, manipulative, or complex contextual scenarios. 
Understanding the nature, boundaries, and limitations of these prompts is crucial to avoiding misinformation or misapplication. Users and researchers are urged to exercise analytical caution and adhere to standard safety guidelines when testing or implementing this content.
